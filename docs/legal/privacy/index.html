G88 Privacy Policy

Last updated: 23 June 2026 · Operated by Valentin Ganchev (“G88”, “we”, “us”).

G88 is a map-first, location-based social app: nearby people appear as avatars on a live map so you can wave, chat, and discover local activity. This policy explains what we collect, how we use it, who we share it with, and the choices and rights you have. Privacy is a core design constraint of the product, not an afterthought — most notably, your exact location is never shared with other users or stored on our servers (see “Location” below).

1. Who this applies to

This policy covers the G88 mobile application and its backend services. By creating an account or using G88 you agree to it. G88 is not directed to children; you must be at least 13 (or the minimum digital-consent age in your country, if higher) to use it.

3. Location — how we protect it

Your device determines your precise GPS position to place you on the map. That precise position is transmitted to our server over an encrypted connection and is immediately coarsened to roughly a 120-meter area (an H3 grid-cell centroid) before anything is stored. We do not retain your exact GPS coordinates, and other users only ever see the coarsened location. This means you appear “in the neighborhood,” never at a precise point.

Location is used only while you are using the app (foreground); G88 does not track your location in the background. You can hide yourself from discovery at any time in Settings → Appear on map, and you can revoke the OS location permission in your device settings (the map will stop working while it is off).

4. How we use your data

  • Operate core features: discovery, presence, waves, chat, events, listings, gifts, challenges.
  • Authenticate you and keep your account secure.
  • Send notifications you have not opted out of (you control these per-channel in Settings).
  • Maintain reliability and diagnose crashes and errors.
  • Understand aggregate product usage to improve G88.
  • Process verification and subscriptions when you choose to use them.

We do not sell your personal data, and we do not show third-party advertising.

Other users see your display name, avatar/photos, bio, interests, badges, and your coarsened (~120 m) location — not your email, phone, or precise location.

6. Data retention

We keep your data while your account is active. Diagnostic and analytics data are retained for a limited period for security and product purposes. When you delete your account, your data is removed as described below. Presence data is ephemeral and expires automatically within minutes.

7. Deleting your account and data

You can permanently delete your account and associated data at any time:

  • In the app: Settings → Delete account. This is immediate and irreversible.
  • By web/email request: email vganchev6@gmail.com from your account email with the subject “Delete my account” and we will complete the deletion.

Deletion permanently removes your profile, photos and uploaded documents, messages, waves, listings, events, gifts, gamification progress, verification records, and authentication sessions. Some records may persist briefly in encrypted backups before being overwritten, and we may retain the minimum needed to comply with legal obligations.

8. Your rights

Depending on where you live (e.g. the EEA/UK under GDPR, or California under the CCPA/CPRA), you may have the right to access, correct, delete, or port your data, to object to or restrict certain processing, and to withdraw consent. You can exercise most of these in the app, or by contacting us at the address below. You also have the right to complain to your local data-protection authority.

9. Security

Data is encrypted in transit (HTTPS/TLS, secure WebSockets). Passwords are stored only as salted hashes; authentication tokens on your device are held in the OS secure keystore. No system is perfectly secure, but we work to protect your information and to minimise what we collect (for example, by coarsening location before storage).

10. International transfers

Our providers may process data in regions including the EU and the US. Where data is transferred internationally, we rely on appropriate safeguards such as standard contractual clauses.

11. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy as the product evolves. Material changes will be reflected by an updated “Last updated” date and, where appropriate, an in-app notice.
